Texas Ranch Water

Refreshing!

Description

One of the best cocktails I've ever made. A light drink that doesn't fill you up with calories!

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces blanco tequila, about 2 shots
  • 1 and a half ounces fresh lime juice
  • Chilled topo chico
  • fresh lime for garnish

Steps

  1. Using a highball glass-really, whatevery glass you have on hand - fill with ice. Add teguila and fresh lime juice. Top with Topo Chico. Add fresh lime wedge to the rim of the glass.
  2. The amount of tequila and lime juice can be halved to fit a smaller cocktail glass or to be less boozy in general. Just use a ratio of 2 parts tequila, one part fresh lime juice.
